Nail Services

Our nail artists are passionate about creating stunning and durable looks. We use only high-quality, professional-grade products to ensure your nails are not only beautiful but also healthy.

Custom Nail Art

From minimalist accents to hand-painted designs, our artists work with you to create a personalized look. Bring your inspiration or let us design something unique.

Gel Manicure & Pedicure

Enjoy a chip-free, high-gloss finish that lasts for weeks. We offer a wide variety of colors and finishes to match your style.

Classic Manicure & Pedicure

A timeless treatment that includes shaping, cuticle care, gentle scrub, and a perfect polish application for a clean, elegant finish.

Eyelash Services

Achieve the luscious, full lashes you’ve always dreamed of with our range of extension and enhancement services. Our certified lash technicians prioritize the health of your natural lashes.

Classic Lash Extensions

One individual extension is applied to each natural lash, creating a subtle and natural enhancement. Perfect for adding length and curl.

Volume & Hybrid Extensions

For a fuller, more dramatic effect, volume sets use lightweight fans of multiple extensions. Hybrid sets offer a beautiful blend of both classic and volume techniques.

Lash Lift & Tint

A semi-permanent treatment that gives your natural lashes a beautiful curl and lift, making them appear longer and fuller. The tint adds a dark, mascara-like effect.

Eyebrow Services

Well-groomed eyebrows frame the face and enhance your features. Our brow experts are skilled in a variety of techniques to create your perfect brow shape and style.

Brow Shaping & Waxing

Our specialists analyze your facial structure to design the ideal brow shape for you, using a gentle wax for precise and clean results.

Brow Lamination

This treatment straightens and sets brow hairs in place, creating a fuller, more defined, and perfectly groomed look that lasts for weeks.

Brow Tinting

Enhance the color and definition of your brows with a custom-blended tint. This service is great for adding fullness and depth to light or sparse eyebrows.
